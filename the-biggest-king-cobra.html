14. The king cobra is the largest venomous snake in the world. It has special muscles and ribs in its neck that spread out to form a "hood" when the cobra feels threatened. This makes the snake look bigger than it really is, and may help scare away predators. King cobras make a deep, loud hissing sound. Like the rattle of the rattlesnake, the hiss is a warning signal that can be heard from a distance. The message is: "I am big, bad, and will bite you if you come any closer!" Most cobras are known to protect their eggs after laying them, but the female king cobra does even more. She uses her body and head to move leaves around to build a nest. After she lays her eggs, she covers them with more leaves, and stays on top of the nest until the eggs hatch.Source: http://kids.sandiegozoo.orgWrite the detailed facts about the king cobra!1. The physical feature :2. Habitat. :3.Diet. :4. The unique. :5.Habit. :​


Jawaban:

1.In addition to their trademark hoods, cobras have round pupils and smooth scales. Colors vary widely from species to species. There are red, yellow, black, mottled, banded and many other colors and patterns of cobra. Cobras are large snakes; many species reach more than 6 feet long (2 meters)

2.King cobras live mainly in the rain forests and plains of India, southern China, and Southeast Asia, and their coloring can vary greatly from region to region. They are comfortable in a variety of habitats, including forests, bamboo thickets, mangrove swamps, high-altitude grasslands, and in rivers.

3.A mouse, rat, ground squirrel, or rabbit are its main prey items, but cobras may also eat amphibians, birds, lizards, other snakes, and eggs. Cobras use their forked tongue to smell prey.

4.  “The most well-known distinctive physical characteristic of cobras is their hood,” said Viernum. “Hooding occurs when the snake spreads out its neck ribs forming a flattened, widened section of its body near the head.” This creates a stunning, and threatening, spectacle

5 .Daily Life: King cobras are active by day and night, but are rarely seen. They hunt by smell, 'tasting' the air with their forked tongues to track their prey. When they get close, they use their eyes to pinpoint their victim before striking, injecting a massive dose of venom.

33. The man was a king.The negative sentence is……a. The man was not a kingb. The man is not a kingc. Was the man a king?d. The man did not a king​


Jawaban:

A. The man wasn't a king

Penjelasan:

Karena, kalau kalimat nya negatif tambahkan kata not di verb nya was jadi ''Wasn't''.

Was adalah bentuk V2 (past tense/lampau) dari is/am (to be), dan were adalah bentuk V2 dari are (to be). Was digunakan untuk subject yang tunggal (satu) dan untuk kata ganti I, he, she, it. Sedangkan were digunakan untuk subject yang jamak (lebih dari satu) dan kata ganti you, they, we.
