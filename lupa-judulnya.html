7. Tolong ya aku kasih 15poin jangan asal jangan lupa kasih judul kak ​


Kenduren

Adat utawi tradisi kenduren inggih menika tradisi ingkang sampun turun termurun saking nenek moyang wonten ing jaman rumiyin. Tradisi kenduren kawontenan ing masyarakat jawa tengah ingkang taksih asring dipunlaksanakaken ngantos sakmenika. Tradisi menika kagungan ancas nyuwun kaslametan dening gusti Allah swt kangge tiyang ingkang ngawontenaken adat menika. Wonten adat menika ingkah kedah dipuncawisaken inggih menika tumpeng jangkep kaliyan lawuhipun. Tumpeng lan lawuhipun dipundongani sesarengan kaliyan para rawuh, ingkang dipunpimpin kaliyan pemuka adat utawi sesepuh adat. Sesampunipun tumpeng kalawau dipundongani, tumpeng wau dipunbageaken ingkang asring dipunsebat carikan utawi berkat.

Adat kenduren menika kathah jinisipun, wonten kenduren wetonan (wedalan) dipunwastani wetonan amargi ancasipun kangge nyuwun kaslemetan menawi dinten kelahiran ( weton, Jawa). Wonten ugi kenduren sabanah (munggahan) kenduren menika kawontenan wonten ing bulan sya'ban. Lajengipun woten kenduren likuran, kenduren menika kalaksanakaken tanggal 21 wulan pasa, kagungan ancas mangeti dinten Nuzulul Qur'an. Ingkang pungkasan inggih menika kenduren badan (lebaran) dipunlaksanakaken dinten idul fitri 1 syawal, anggadhahi ancas kangge ngandapaken para leluhur. Sinaosa mecemipun kathah, nanging kenduren menika ancasipun sami, inggih menika nyuwun kaslametan dening Gusti Allah.

18. Ciri kebahasaan teks cerpen yang berjudul gara gara nenek lupa

Once upon a time, in a small village nestled amidst rolling green hills, lived a young girl named Sarah. She had a heart full of curiosity and a thirst for adventure. Sarah's life was filled with ordinary days, but she always longed for something extraordinary to happen.

One sunny morning, as Sarah was exploring the forest near her village, she stumbled upon an old, dusty book hidden beneath a moss-covered rock. Intrigued, she opened it and discovered that it contained a map leading to a hidden treasure.

Excitement surged through Sarah's veins as she embarked on a thrilling treasure hunt. With the map in hand, she followed the cryptic clues that took her through dense forests, across sparkling rivers, and up towering mountains. Along the way, she encountered various challenges, but her determination never wavered.

As Sarah ventured deeper into the journey, she met new friends who joined her in the quest for the treasure. Together, they formed an unbreakable bond, supporting and encouraging each other through thick and thin. Their shared laughter and camaraderie made the journey even more memorable.

After days of searching, Sarah and her newfound companions finally reached the spot indicated on the map. It was a hidden cave, tucked away behind a magnificent waterfall. But amidst the glittering treasures, it was the journey itself and the friendships forged that truly made the experience unforgettable for Sarah. She realized that the treasure she sought wasn't just material wealth but the memories and bonds that would last a lifetime.

As years went by, Sarah cherished the memories of that adventure, looking back on it as a defining moment in her life. The treasure hunt taught her the value of perseverance, friendship, and the beauty of the unknown. It inspired her to continue seeking new experiences and embracing the magic of life.

And so, Sarah carried the spirit of that unforgettable journey in her heart, forever grateful for the memories created and the lessons learned. The treasure may have been left behind, but the impact it had on her life was immeasurable.
