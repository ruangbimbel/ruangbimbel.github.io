20. Is "my favorite celebrity" is a noun phrase?


Yes, it is.

A noun phrase, or nominal, is a phrase that has a noun or pronoun as its head or performs the same grammatical function as a noun. Examples:
•the little boy.
•the happy puppy.
•the building on the corner.
•the sharp pencil.
•your religion.

33. Tulislah 10 kata sifat dan artinya lalu masing masing buatlah contoh kalimat nominal dengan menggunakan kata sifat tersebut. Contoh - smart : cerdas He is a smart student... atau He was a smart student.. atau He is so smart... atau He was smart​


Jawaban:

. Kata Kerja (Verb)

- eat (makan)

- drink (minum)

- go (pergi)

- sing (menyanyi)

- choose (memilih)

- bite (menggigit)

- read (membaca)

- throw (melempar)

etc.

Contoh dalam kalimat :

- My mother sings in the karaoke box. (Ibuku menyanyi di dalam kotak karaoke.)

- Sule and Andre eat a bowl of Samyang together. (Sule dan Andre makan satu mangkuk Samyang bersama.)

2. Kata Benda (Noun)

a. Countable Nouns

- bridge (jembatan)

- apple (apel)

- photo frame (bingkai foto)

- ghost (hantu)

- tile (genteng)

- pillow (bantal)

Contoh kalimat :

- I have two apples in the refrigerator. (Saya mempunyai dua buah apel di kulkas)

b. Uncountable Nouns

- water (air)

- money (uang)

- milk (susu)

- tea (teh)

Contoh kalimat :

- I drink a bottle of milk every morning. (Saya minum satu botol susu setiap pagi.)

3. Kata Sifat (Adjective)

- nice (baik)

- good (bagus)

- beautiful (indah)

- handsome (tampan)

- old (tua)

- smart (cerdas)

- diligent (rajin)

- cute (imut)

Contoh kalimat :

- Haruka is a cute girl. (Haruka adalah gadis yang imut)

4. Kata Keterangan (Adverb)

a. Adverb of time

- in the morning (di pagi hari)

- midnight (tengah malam)

- now (sekarang)

- before dark (sebelum gelap/malam)

b. Adverb of place

- at school (di sekolah)

- on the island (di pulau)

c. Adverb of manner

- quietly (dengan diam)

- impatiently (dengan tidak sabar)

d. Adverb of frequency

- never (tidak pernah)

- often (sering)

- everyday (setiap hari)

- every morning (setiap pagi)

e. Adverb of purpose

- to get a new book. (untuk mendapatkan sebuah buku baru)

- to avoid long queue (untuk menghindari antrian panjang)
