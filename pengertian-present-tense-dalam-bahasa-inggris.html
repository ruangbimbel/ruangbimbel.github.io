Simple present tense is a basic grammar which used to state facts which don't change, or events that happen routinely (repeated actions). Simple present tense devided into two kind of sentences, verbal and nominal. Each sentence has three forms (positive, negative, and interrogative)

3. pengertian present continuous tense menggunakan bahasa inggris


Present Continuous is used to express a continuing action. This verb form is used to show that something is happening or will be happening very soon. They are used with active action words in the form of present participles ending in "-ing".

7. Apa itu simple present tense?

JAWABAN

Simple present tense adalah sebuah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta, kebiasaan, dan kebenaran umum. Rumusnya adalah sebagai berikut!

[Kalimat Verba]

(+) S + V1/Vs + O

(-) S + don't/doesn't + V1 + O

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O?

[Kalimat Nominal]

(+) S + is/am/are + C

(-) S + is/am/are + not + C

(?) Is/Am/Are + S + C?

*S = Subject; V = Verb; O = Object; C = Complement.

13. jelaskan mempresentasikan simple present tense dengan lengkap dari pembuka sampai akhir contoh kalimatnya, rumusnya, kata kerjanya, kata bantu pokoknya lengkap pakai bahasa inggris sama pengertiannya juga


Simple present tense is a verb form to express facts, habits, or events that occur at this time.   This form of verb is most commonly used in English.

Pattern :
 
(+) Positive
: Subject + verb 1 (+ s/es) + object

: Subject + to be (am/is/are) + adjective/adverb

Example :

· I use social media

· She is very polite and kind

(-) Negative
: Subject + do/does not + verb 1 + …

: Subject + to be (am/is/are) + not + adjective/adverb

Example :

· They do not use social media

· She is not very polite and kind

(?)
: Do/does + subject + verb 1 + object?

: To be (am/is/are) + subject + adjective/adverb?

Example :

· Does he use social media?

· Is she polite and kind?
