Pembahasan

Kata kerja dalam bahasa Inggris disebut dengan verbs. Kata kerja atau verb menunjukkan aksi, tindakan, dan hal yang dilakukan oleh subjek. Dalam bahasa Inggris, ada 3 jenis verba yaitu verb base form, verb simple past, dan verb past participle.

Kata kerja present merupakan kata kerja yang digunakan dalam present tense. Kata kerja present tense merupakan verb 1 atau base form. Kata kerja present menunjukkan tindakan yang dilakukan merupakan tindakan saat ini, kebiasaan, dan merupakan sebuah fakta.

Kata kerja past merupakan kata kerja yang digunakan dalam past tense. Kata kerja past merupakan verb 2 dan verb 3 atau biasa disebut dengan past simple dan past participle. Kata kerja past menunjukkan kegiatan yang telah dilakukan atau kegiatan di masa lampau.

19. Membandingkan waktu dulu dan waktu sekarang dalam past tens


Past Tense and Present Tense - Then and Now

1. Past: She was in Grade VII last year.

Present: She is in Grade VIII now.

2. Past: She learned many new things.

Present: She learns more things now.

3. Past: She rode to school on her bike for the first time.

Present: She rides her bike to school now.

4. Past: She met boys and girls from the different parts of the town for the first time.

Present: She plays with them now.

5. Past: She was very happy when she wore the blue and white uniform for the first time.

Present: She wears them now.

6. Past: She learned English for the first time last year.

Present: She learns English now.

7. Past: She learned to greet other people in English.

Present: She can greet other people in English now.

8. Past: She learned to introduce herself in English.

Present: She can introduce herself in English now.

9. Past: She learned to describe her family, her friends, and her school in English.

Present: She can describe her family, her friends, and her school in English now.

10. Past: She sang her first English song.

Present: She can sing in English now.

11. Past: She read her first English story.

Present: She can read her stories in English now.

12. Past: She was not a class captain.

Present: She is a class captain now.

13. Past: She did not do many things for her class.

Present: She does many things for her class.

Pembahasan

Simple past tense adalah kalimat yang menunjukan keterangan waktu di masa lalu. Berikut adalah formulanya:

(+) Subject + V2

I watched TV last weekHe met his wife 10 years ago

(-) Subject + did NOT + V1

She didn’t finish the exercise yesterdayI didn’t go to school last week

(?) Did + Subject + V1?

Did you see me yesterday?Did Bella take her medicine?

Simple past tense of to be, to have, to do

I, He/She/It => was, had, did

You, We, They => were, had, did

Simple present adalah tense yang menunjukan kegiatan yang terjadi saat ini (present) maupun kebiasaan yang terjadi pada waktu tertantu secara rutin. Berikut adalah formulanya:

(+) Subject + V1 (-s, -es, -ies)

He goes to schoolMy daughter lives in South AfricaIt snows a lot during the winter

Subject: (He, She, It)

(-) Subject + does/do NOT + V1

My mother doesn’t speak good englishIt doesn’t rain in the summerShe doesn’t study Math on Monday

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)

(?) Does/Do + Subject + V1

Does he go to school?Does she live in Paris?Do you like apple?

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday ?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist ?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning ?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils  ?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent ?

10. (+) My father was in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office  yesterday afternoon?
