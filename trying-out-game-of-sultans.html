PEMBAHASAN MATERI✎ Definisi Simple Present Tense

Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan:

- Suatu fakta, misalnya:

Matahari terbit di timurMatahari tenggelam di barat

- Suatu pekerjaan yang dilakukan secara berulang-ulang atau sudah menjadi suatu kebiasaan, misalnya:

Saya makan nasi tiga kali sehariDia berangkat sekolah pukul 7 pagi setiap hari_____________________➽ Pola Simple Present Tense(+) ➜ S + V1 + O + C(–) ➜ S + do/does + not + V1 + O + C(?) ➜ Do/does + S + V1 + O + C_____________________➽ Catatan

Do digunakan untuk subjek

I (saya)you (kamu/kalian)we (kami)they (mereka)nama orang banyak (misal: Emdy dan Thian)

Does digunakan untuk subjek

he (dia laki-laki)she (dia perempuan)it (dia/itu (kata ganti hewan, benda, tumbuhan))nama orang tunggal (misal: Thian)_____________________➽ Contoh Penambahan -s/-es Pada Kata Kerja (Verb)

21. Many people had been trying that game jadikan passive voice


Bentuk passive dari "Many people had been trying that game" adalah...

Dilihat dari struktur kalimat nya, kalimat di atas termasuk ke dalam PAST PERFECT CONTINUOUS, dimana rumus nya :
Subject + had + been + V-ing + object.
=> Many people (had been trying) that game.

Maka rumus passive nya adalah..
Subject + had + been + being + V3 + by...

Catatan !!!
* subject di kalimat aktif berubah jadi object di kalimat pasif, begitu pula sebaliknya.

Maka jawabannya adalah..
=> That game HAD BEEN BEING TRIED by many people.
===============================
Mapel : Bahasa Inggris
Materi : Passive Voice
Kelas : 9
Kode Soal : 9.5
Kode Kategorisasi : 9.5.4
Kata Kunci : Past Perfect ContinuousJawaban:
That game had been being tried by many people

Berdasarkan soal, kalimat tersebut adalah kalimat Past Perfect Continues yg memiliki pola;
S + had + been + V+ing + O
active: Many people had been trying that game

Maka perubahan ke Passive;
S + had been + being + V3 + by (agent)
passive:
That game had been being tried by many people
