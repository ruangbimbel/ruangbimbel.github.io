40. Task 3 Arrange these words to make good english 1.understand-you-lyrics-should-the 2.littet-they-must not- 3.low-keep-their voice-they-must-


Jawaban:You should understand the lyric They must not litter They must keep their voice low

PEMBAHASAN Perintah soal

Task 3 Arrange these words to make good english

=> Susunlah kata-kata menjadi kalimat yg benar

No. 1 “Suggestion”

Digunakan untuk menyatakan saran untuk melakukan sesuatu. Rumus:

(+) S + should + V1 + O(-) S + should + not + V1 + O(?) should + S + V1 + O?

1.understand-you-lyrics-should-the

=> You should understand the lyric

Artinya: kamu harus memahami liriknya

No. 2 dan 3 “Obligation”

Digunakan untuk menyatakan larangan melakukan atau mewajibkan suatu tindakan. Rumus:

(+) S + must + V1 (-) S + must + not + V1 (?) must + S + V1?

2.littet-they-must not-

=> They must not litter

Artinya: Mereka tidak boleh membuang sampah sembarangan

3.low-keep-their voice-they-must-

=> They must keep their voice low

Artinya: mereka harus menjaga suara mereka tetap kecil
