16. what is the movement used in rugby​


Jawaban:

In rugby, there are various types of movements and techniques used by players during a match. Some of the common movements and actions include:

1. **Running:** Players carry the ball while running forward to advance toward the opponent's goal line. This often involves evading tacklers and using speed and agility.

2. **Passing:** Players pass the ball laterally or backward to teammates to maintain possession and advance down the field. Passing can be done with both hands and can vary in distance.

3. **Kicking:** Players may kick the ball strategically to gain field position, put pressure on the opposing team, or attempt to score points through kicks at the goalposts. There are different types of kicks, including punts, grubber kicks, and drop kicks.

4. **Tackling:** Defenders use tackling techniques to stop the ball carrier by bringing them to the ground. Tackling is a fundamental aspect of rugby and requires proper technique to ensure player safety.

5. **Scrum:** In rugby union, a scrum is a method of restarting play after a minor infringement or stoppage. It involves the forward packs of both teams binding together and pushing against each other while the ball is fed into the scrum.

6. **Lineout:** A lineout is a method of restarting play after the ball has gone out of bounds. Players from both teams line up and lift teammates to catch a thrown-in ball.

7. **Maul:** A maul occurs when a ball carrier is held by one or more opponents and one or more of the ball carrier's teammates bind onto the ball carrier. It's a way to gain ground as a group.

8. **Rucking:** After a tackle, players from both teams compete for possession of the ball on the ground by attempting to drive the opposing players away from it using their feet.

9. **Support Play:** Players work together to provide support to the ball carrier, ensuring that there are options for passing or recycling the ball quickly.

These are just some of the key movements and actions used in rugby. The specific movements and strategies employed can vary depending on the situation, team tactics, and the format of the game (e.g., rugby union or rugby league). Rugby is a dynamic sport that requires a combination of physical skills, teamwork, and strategy.

21. what is the movement used in tennis​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

In tennis, several types of movements are commonly used by players to navigate the court and hit the ball effectively. These movements include:

1. **Running:** Players need to run quickly and efficiently to reach the ball before it bounces twice. Short sprints, side-to-side movements, and rapid changes in direction are common in tennis.

2. **Sideways Shuffle:** This movement involves shuffling the feet sideways along the baseline, allowing the player to maintain a balanced and ready position to react to the ball.

3. **Lunging:** Lunging involves a rapid forward step, often accompanied by a split step or jump, to reach the ball close to the net or cover a wide area of the court. This movement requires balance and quick reaction time.

4. **Backpedaling:** Players often need to backpedal when the ball is hit deep into the court. Backpedaling allows players to maintain their position and prepare for the next shot.

5. **Crossing Over:** In situations where players need to switch directions rapidly, they may use the crossover step, where one foot crosses in front of the other to change direction efficiently.

6. **Volleying:** In addition to moving to reach the ball, players also need to volley the ball, hitting it in the air before it bounces. Volleying requires precise timing and hand-eye coordination.

7. **Serving:** The serve involves a specialized movement where the player throws the ball into the air and hits it to initiate a point. The serve requires power, accuracy, and technique.

8. **Smashing:** Smashing is an overhead shot near the net where players jump and strike the ball with force. This movement is used to return high shots effectively.

9. **Drop Shot and Lob:** These are specific shots in tennis that involve controlling the height and placement of the ball with finesse. Players may adjust their movements and shot selection to execute these tactics.

10. **Approach and Retreat:** Players often use this movement to approach the net when they want to dominate the point or retreat to the baseline when they need to defend.

Effective movement in tennis is crucial for maintaining control of the game, reaching the ball in time, and conserving energy. Players often practice these movements intensively to improve their on-court performance.

23. what is the pattern of cause and effect in sentence


CAUSE AND EFFECT

what is the pattern of cause and effect in sentence

JAWABAN:

There are two sentence structures of cause and effect:

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + noun Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subject + verb

PEMBAHASAN:

=> Apa pola sebab dan akibat dalam kalimat?

Ada dua pola kalimat dari cause and effect:

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + kata benda Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subjek + kata kerja

Contoh kalimat cause and effect:

Due to his good personality, he was elected as exemplary student. (Karena kepribadiannya yang baik, ia terpilih sebagai siswa teladan)

Cause : his good personality

Effect : he was elected as exemplary student

Signal word : Due to

Because of you, I will stay here until the end of the time. (Karena kamu, saya akan tinggal di sini sampai akhir waktu)

Cause : you

Effect : I will stay here until the end of the time

Signal word : Because of

She became really ashamed owing to the incident in the stage when she forgot the lyrics of the song. (Dia menjadi sangat malu karena insiden di panggung ketika dia lupa lirik lagu tersebut)

Cause : he incident in the stage when she forgot the lyrics of the song

Effect : She became really ashamed

Signal word : owing to  

I can continue my study abroad easily thanks to your guidance for me. (Saya dapat melanjutkan studi saya di luar negeri dengan mudah berkat bimbingan Anda untuk saya)

Cause : your guidance for me

Effect : I can continue my study abroad easily

Signal word : thanks to

Ammar studies hard every day because he wants to be a good doctor someday. (Ammar belajar keras setiap hari karena dia ingin menjadi dokter yang baik suatu hari nanti)

Cause : he wants to be a good doctor someday

Effect : Ammar studies hard every day

Signal word : because

Since Ameera was sick yesterday, she didn't come to school. (Karena Ameera sakit kemarin, dia tidak datang ke sekolah)

Cause : Ameera was sick yesterday

Effect : she didn't come to school

Signal word : since

She got so many gift from her friends as it was her birthday. (Dia mendapat banyak hadiah dari teman-temannya karena itu adalah hari ulang tahunnya)

Cause : it was her birthday

Effect : She got so many gift from her friends

Signal word : as

I felt very boring for I was waiting for my fiend in the air port. (Saya merasa sangat bosan karena saya sedang menunggu teman saya di bandara)

Cause : I was waiting for my fiend in the air port

Effect : I felt very boring

Signal word : for

I could not go to Bandung this morning as a result of I was late . (Saya tidak bisa pergi ke Bandung pagi ini karena saya terlambat)

Cause : I was late

Effect : I could not go to Bandung this morning

Signal word : as a result of

Jawaban:

Can you tell what pattern is written in bold type?

Yes, I can. It is simple past tense. So, the pattern in bold type is

Positive : Subject + past verb (verb 2)

Negative: Subject + auxiliary verb did + not + present verb (verb 1)

Interrogative : Auxiliary verb did + subject + present verb (verb 1)?

Penjelasan:

What about the pattern in the bold italic type?

It is present perfect tense. So, the pattern in the bold italic type is

Positive : subject + have/has + past participle verb (verb 3)

Negative : subject + have/has + not + past participle verb (verb 3)

Interrogative : Have/has + subject + past participle verb (verb 3)?
