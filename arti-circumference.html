35. The area of a circle is 38.5cm² Find its circumference. (Taken π to be 22/7 .)​


Answer:

22 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Let the radius of circular disc be R

Area of Circular Disc = 38.5 cm²

We know that Area of Circle = π(Radius)²

∴ π × R² = 38.5

22/7 × R² = 38.5

R² = (38.5 × 7)/22

R² = 3.5 × 3.5

R² = 3.5²

R = 3.5 cm

Now, Circumference of Circle = 2π×Radius

∴ Circumference of given Circular Disc = 2 × (22/7) × 3.5

                                                                 = 22 cm
