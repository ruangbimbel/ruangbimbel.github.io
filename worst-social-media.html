15. buatlah Descriptive text tentang social media ​


Jawaban:

Just like the name, social media is a tool on the internet that you can use to keep in touch with your social circle. It is also a place for you to share anything about your social life. There are so many kinds of social media such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube, Ask.fm and also LinkedIn. Each of them has its own unique feature and functions. Society, especially teenager, is now clinging tightly to social media. It is through social media that they receive an update about the world and it is on social media they currently build their life and career. This condition creates a huge change in the society. Regardless of whether or not this change is good, some people are actually concern about this matter and start to think of the pros and cons of social media thoroughly.

There is no doubt about the benefit of using social media. The simplicity that we experience to keep in touch with our friends or relatives is definitely so precious. More importantly, as it links every users through the internet, its coverage is worldwide which means we can still connect to our beloved ones even though he or she is currently in a holiday in another country. And the best part is, it doesn’t take any additional cost. It is completely free. Just imagine how much money that we can save from this feature only.

Stream of information is another huge advantage of social media. With the development of social media, it is now more than just a tool to communicate with someone. There are so many accounts, such as on Instagram and Twitter, who post some up-to-date news on daily basis. This way, all active users of social media will be able to keep up with the news of the world.

Social media also provides opportunities for a lot of people to make a living. It is a common knowledge that nowadays there are a lot of people making actual income through social media by becoming a superstar on it such as Instagram celebrity or Vlogger on YouTube. Besides, a professionals social media such as LinkedIn allow its users to build a social profile and connect it with companies around the world who might required their talents and services. It means that social media open up various opportunities for a lot of people to make a living through it.

In the other side, with all amazing features that a social media has, it can also be considered as too attractive that it can lead to something negative at the end. It is no longer a secret that nowadays our young generations have a very strong attachment toward social media. They spend hours in a day just to keep scrolling on their Smartphone screen checking new feeds on their social media account. Some of them even cannot live a day without it. This level of addiction can be dangerous for them, because they may have wasted hours of their precious time without they even realize which may cost them their health.

Additionally, some recent research on social media found out that social media have negative effect on mental health of teenagers. One of the most common effects is the FOMO syndrome. FOMO syndrome stands for “Fear Of Missing Out” which means that someone has the anxiety of being left behind and not knowing the latest update of anything around them. This condition keeps their brain in alert mode almost all the time which will affect their physical health at the end. It is proven by the research from the University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine which show us that teenagers who spend most of their time on social media are more likely to experience sleeping problem and depression.

Social media may have its benefit for its users but if the users are not smart enough to protect themselves, social media can also be dangerous for them. As the users of social media, we need to consider our action when we want to use it, because no matter what happen at the end, social media is merely a tool, we are the one who have control over it.

17. apa hubungan social media dan cyberspace?


Jawaban:

Waktu menunjukkan pukul 10 pagi, Munib (bukan nama sebenarnya) masih saja tergeletak santai, tidur di kamar kontrakan sembari menatap lekat gawainya yang sama sekali tidak ia lepaskan sejak bangun tidur tadi. Barang tentu, ia belum makan apalagi mandi pagi yang katanya justru menyehatkan. Ia masih saja tenggelam dalam dunia virtual instagram, whatsapp, dan sesekali membuka Youtube dan melihat apa saja yang ditemuinya di gawai tersebut. Tentu saja kejadian ini tidak hanya terjadi pada seorang Munib, melainkan menjadi fenomena yang sering kita temui di sudut-sudut warung kopi hingga ruang akademis yang seringkali abai terhadap kehidupan nyata.

Dewasa ini, dunia Cyberspace menjadi Common reality, bentuk baru perkembangan hiperrealitas. Perkembangannya pun tidak dapat dilepaskan dari pondasi-pondasi nilai filosofis, etis, ideologis. Berbagai bentuk imajinasi dan visi yang semula hanya merupakan angan-angan, kini terbungkus pada kain harapan bernama “realitas”.

Masalah pun muncul, ketika nilai-nilai yang bersifat partikular ditawarkan di dalam dunia Cyberspace mengakibatkan nilai-nilai saling berkontradiksi satu sama lain dengan masyarakat pengguna. Maka, timbullah sebuah distorsi nilai ideologi, etika dan nilai-nilai budaya yang berbeda dengan pandangan dunia masyarakat pengguna.

Lalu, fenomena ini menanggalkan pertanyaan paling jujur, “untuk siapa sifat komunikatif cyberspacedalam mendulang banyak kekuatan massa?” untuk menjawab hal tersebut perlulah kiranya taksonomi skema maupun struktur berbntuk klasifikasi terhadap fenomena Cyberspace media sosial.

Mengacu pada istilah definitif Strinati (2009)terhadap peristilahan budaya massa sebagai platform yang menaungi media sosial. Jika budaya massa merupakan budaya popular yang dihasilkan melalui teknik-teknik industrial produksi massa dan dipasarkan untuk mendapatkan keuntungan kepada khalayak konsumen, maka media sosial merupakan platform yang sengaja dibuat dalam menunjang kegiatan tersebut, terutama dalam melakukan bentuk komunikasi.Bahkan, Wattimena melalui tulisannya dalam “Menggagas CyberSpace Sebagai Ruang Publik Virtual Yang Emansipatif.” melihat sebagai bentuk baru imperialisme barat dalam bentuk neo liberalisme serta sebagai kekuatan baru masyarakat sipil yang luar biasa.

Redana dalam Potret Manusia Sebagai Si Anak Kebudayaan Massa menyebutkan bahwa Cyberspace merupakan realitas gadungan, dunia fiksi yang tidak jelas batasnya, dunia bebas tanpa sekat yang menjadikan masyarakat yang belum sepenuhnya, lebur di rekstrukturisasi oleh media sosial. Lalu menyebutnya sebagai zaman edan.

35. Menurut anda apakah social media memajukan ekonomi?


Social media dapat membantu memperluas jangkauan pemasaran suatu bisnis dengan biaya yang relatif murah. Bisnis dapat memanfaatkan social media untuk mempromosikan produk atau jasa mereka secara online dan menjangkau konsumen potensial dari berbagai wilayah.Social media dapat meningkatkan interaksi dan keterlibatan pelanggan dengan bisnis. Melalui social media, bisnis dapat berinteraksi dengan pelanggan, menjawab pertanyaan mereka, dan menerima umpan balik dari konsumen. Hal ini dapat membantu bisnis untuk lebih memahami kebutuhan dan keinginan pelanggan mereka, serta meningkatkan loyalitas pelanggan.Social media dapat membuka peluang baru bagi bisnis untuk menjalin kerjasama dengan pihak lain, seperti influencer atau partner bisnis lainnya. Dengan menjalin kemitraan melalui social media, bisnis dapat memperluas jangkauan dan menambah nilai tambah pada produk atau jasa yang mereka tawarkan.
