The students exam will done20. is - lunch – today - provided - beinga. lunch is being provided todayb. is being lunch provided todayC. today is being provided lunchd. lunch is provided being today​


PASSIVE VOICE (KALIMAT PASIF)

Passive sentence adalah kalimat yang subjeknya dikenai tindakan/pekerjaan. Biasanya kata kerja dari kalimat ini diartikan menjadi "di" atau "ter"

Rumus kalimat : Subjek + aux.verb + Verb III/Past participle

Contoh kalimat passive voice :

1. Coffe is made by Febry

2. Milk tea was being made by Aqilla

3. Juice apple can be made John
