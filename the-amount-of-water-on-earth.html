7. The gravitational force of a satellite on the surface of the Earth of radius R is F. What is the gravitational force on the satellite when its height is R above the Earth?​


Jawaban:

F/4

Penjelasan:

h = distance from earth core to satellite

h = R+R = 2R

Fb = gravitational force on the satellite when its height is R above the Earth

F = G m / R^2

Fb = GM m / (2R)2

Fb = GM m / 4R2

Fb = (GM m / R2) / 4

Fb = F/4

Jawaban:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

pot = 2 × bottle

pot + bottle = 5 liters

substitute pot with x and bottle with y.

x = 2y

x + y = 5

substitute x with 2y into the equation.

x + y = 5

2y + y = 5

3y = 5

y = 5/3 liters

the amount of water inside the bottle was 5/3 liters.

x = 2y

x = 2(5/3)

x = 10/3

the amount of water inside the pot was 10/3 liters.

15. 5. Calculate the amount of heat needed to raise the temperature of 1.5 kg water from 300C to 800C. (specific heat of water= 1 kcal/kg0C)


Jawaban:

750.000 kal = 750 kkal

Penjelasan:

Diketahui :

m = 1,5 kg

c = 1 kkal/(kg °C)

ΔT = T2 - T1 = 800°C - 300°C = 500°C

Ditanya : Q = ... kal?

Jawab :

Q = m c ΔT

Q = 1,5 kg (1 kkal/(kg °C)) (500°C)

Q = 750 kkal

Q = 750.000 kal
