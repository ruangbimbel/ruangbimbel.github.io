1. Apa arti kata “figure this out” ? Dan apa perbedaan antara : “I can figure this out” dengan “I can figure it out”


Jawaban:

Kata "figure this out" dalam bahasa Inggris memiliki arti "menemukan cara untuk menyelesaikan atau memahami sesuatu".

Perbedaan antara "I can figure this out" dan "I can figure it out" adalah pada penggunaan kata ganti. "This" merujuk pada sesuatu yang spesifik, sedangkan "it" merujuk pada sesuatu yang lebih umum. Oleh karena itu, "I can figure this out" berarti bahwa seseorang percaya bahwa mereka dapat menemukan cara untuk menyelesaikan atau memahami sesuatu yang spesifik, sedangkan "I can figure it out" berarti bahwa seseorang percaya bahwa mereka dapat menemukan cara untuk menyelesaikan atau memahami sesuatu yang lebih umum.

Pembahasan

Macam dan Contoh Sub-ordinate Conjunction

1. time (waktu)  

after (setelah) as (ketika, sementara) as soon as (segera setelah) before (sebelum) once (segera setelah) since (sejak) until/till (sampai) when (ketika) whenever (kapan saja, sewaktu-waktu) while (ketika, sementara)

2. place (tempat)

where (di mana) wherever (di manapun)

3. contrast (pertentangan)

although, even though, though (meskipun) whereas (sebaliknya, sedangkan) while (meskipun) even if (kalaupun)

4. cause and effect (sebab dan akibat)

as (karena) because (karena) since (karena) so (sehingga)

5. condition (syarat)

in order that (agar) so that (agar)

6. purpose (tujuan)

as long as (jika, dengan syarat) if (jika, dengan syarat) only if (hanya jika) otherwise (jika tidak) or else (jika tidak) provided /providing (that) (jika, dengan syarata) supposing (asumsikan, anggap) unless (jika tidak)

7. manner (cara)

as if (seperti) as though (seperti) how (bagaimana) the way (cara)

20. Many people had been trying that game jadikan passive voice


Bentuk passive dari "Many people had been trying that game" adalah...

Dilihat dari struktur kalimat nya, kalimat di atas termasuk ke dalam PAST PERFECT CONTINUOUS, dimana rumus nya :
Subject + had + been + V-ing + object.
=> Many people (had been trying) that game.

Maka rumus passive nya adalah..
Subject + had + been + being + V3 + by...

Catatan !!!
* subject di kalimat aktif berubah jadi object di kalimat pasif, begitu pula sebaliknya.

Maka jawabannya adalah..
=> That game HAD BEEN BEING TRIED by many people.
===============================
Mapel : Bahasa Inggris
Materi : Passive Voice
Kelas : 9
Kode Soal : 9.5
Kode Kategorisasi : 9.5.4
Kata Kunci : Past Perfect ContinuousJawaban:
That game had been being tried by many people

Berdasarkan soal, kalimat tersebut adalah kalimat Past Perfect Continues yg memiliki pola;
S + had + been + V+ing + O
active: Many people had been trying that game

Maka perubahan ke Passive;
S + had been + being + V3 + by (agent)
passive:
That game had been being tried by many people

Perintah pada soal ini adalah "In the following sentences, choose the form in parentheses that means the same as the word or words lying in the inverted commas."

A. Kata figure out kurang tepat karena kalimat ini berbentuk past tense (told).

B. Kata figuring out berbentuk present participle dan kurang tepat karena tidak ada auxiliary verb 'was'.

C. Kata pointed out berarti menemukan.

D. Kata pointing out kurang tepat karena tidak ada auxiliary verb.

Communication is a crucial aspect of human life as it enables individuals to convey messages, share information, and express their thoughts, feelings, and ideas. Here are three reasons why communication is important:

Building relationships: Communication is a fundamental aspect of building relationships. Without communication, it is difficult for individuals to understand each other's needs, wants, and expectations. Effective communication helps to foster positive relationships, whether it is in personal or professional settings.

Problem-solving: Communication is essential for solving problems and conflicts. Clear communication helps to identify issues and find solutions that are agreeable to all parties involved. Personal and professional growth: Communication plays an important role in personal and professional growth. When writing about a famous figure, it is important to provide a detailed and accurate description of their physical appearance, personality, and achievements. Here are some tips on how to write a good description:

Start with the basics: Begin by providing information about the person's name, age, nationality, and profession. This will provide the reader with a basic understanding of who the person is.

Physical appearance: Provide a detailed description of the person's physical appearance, including their height, weight, hair color, eye color, and any distinguishing features such as scars or tattoos.

34. Combine the sentences below using "who" the people are trying to purchase a new phone. They are waiting out side the st.


Relative Pronoun

Combine the sentences below using "who" the people are trying to purchase a new phone. They are waiting out side the street.

Jawab:

The people are trying to purchase a new phone.

They are waiting out side the street.

The people who are waiting outside the street are trying to purchase a new phone.

Kata 'who' diatas digunakan untuk menjelaskan subyek noun yaitu the people.

Pembahasan

Kata who diatas merupakan relative pronouns. Relative pronoun adalah kata atau kalimat yang digunakan untuk menggabungkan kalimat dan untuk menjelaskan noun atau pronoun.

Relative pronoun contohnya adalah who, whose, which, whom dan that. Perbedaan pengunaan relative pronouns tersebut adalah :

who untuk menjelaskan orang (subyek)whom digunakan untuk menjelaskan orang (obyek)whose digunakan untuk menjelaskan kepemilikan biasanya digunakan untuk situasi formalwhich digunakan untuk orang/animals/bendathat digunakan untuk orang/animals/benda dan biasanya digunakan untuk situasi informal

Berikut contoh penggunaan relative pronoun dalam kalimat :

I don't like people who interrupted me.She is the student who handwriting is the best in the class.Did you see the woman which wearing a black dress?The boy who likes pizza is her son.That the boy whose mother is on the TV.Pelajari Lebih Lanjut
