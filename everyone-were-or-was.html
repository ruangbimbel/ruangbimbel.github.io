But you need to remember, that everyone can be followed by two of them.
--> You may use "were" when you are facing subjunctive/conditional form, for example :
"if everyone were happy, I would appreciate on them"
Otherwise, when stating factual statements, it should be "everyone is (or was) present", example --> everyone is great today / everyone was great.

everyone except me was, --> because it's factual statements, however it can be replaced by "were" if it was conditional sentence.

32. work in pairs put for true sentences and put for the false sentencesno 10: (a)everyone except she was coming to the wedding caremony of my brother(b)everyone excepther were coming to the wedding ceremony of my brother(c)everyone except her was coming to the wedding ceremony of mu brothertolong dong ganplisssgbu


Pembahasan

1. a. It’s up to he, my father (FALSE)

b. It’s up to him, my father (TRUE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan he => him.


2. a. It was her, my sister. (TRUE)

b. It was she, my sister. (FALSE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan she => her.


3. a. We talked to Rudy and him. (TRUE)

b. We talked to he and Rudy. (FALSE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan he => him.


4. a. It is I. (FALSE)

b. It is me. (TRUE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan I => me.


5. a. Talk to Steven and they before making a decision (FALSE)

b. Talk to Steven and them before making a decision (TRUE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan they => them.


6. a. Can us, hikers, go with you? (FALSE)

b. Can we, hikers, go with you? (TRUE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi subjek maka tetap menggunakan we.


7. a. Saleha and she have quit the team. (TRUE)

b. Saleha and her have quit the team. (FALSE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi subjek maka tetap menggunakan she.


8. a. They asked him and I to join the family gathering (FALSE)

b. They asked he and me to join the family gathering (FALSE)

c. They asked him and me to join the family gathering (TRUE)

d. They asked he and I to join the family gathering (FALSE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan he => him, I => me.


9. a. That caller was me, not him. (TRUE)

b. That caller was I, not he. (FALSE)

c. That caller was me, not he. (FALSE)

d. That caller was I, not him. (FALSE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan I => me, he => him.


10. a. Everyone except she was coming to the wedding ceremony of my brother. (FALSE)

b. Everyone except her were coming to the wedding ceremony of my brother (FALSE)

c. Everyone except her was coming to the wedding ceremony of my brother (TRUE)

Untuk kata ganti orang yang berada di posisi objek maka menggunakan she => her.

Everyone merupakan bentuk singular (tunggal), oleh karena itu seharusnya menggunakan was, bukan were.
