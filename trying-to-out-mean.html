Untuk menggabungkan 2 kalimat tersebut menjadi 1 kalimat kita membutuhkan sub-ordinate conjunction. Apa itu sub-ordinate conjunction? Simak pembahasan berikut ya..

Pembahasan

Macam dan Contoh Sub-ordinate Conjunction

1. time (waktu)  

after (setelah) as (ketika, sementara) as soon as (segera setelah) before (sebelum) once (segera setelah) since (sejak) until/till (sampai) when (ketika) whenever (kapan saja, sewaktu-waktu) while (ketika, sementara)

2. place (tempat)

where (di mana) wherever (di manapun)

3. contrast (pertentangan)

although, even though, though (meskipun) whereas (sebaliknya, sedangkan) while (meskipun) even if (kalaupun)

4. cause and effect (sebab dan akibat)

as (karena) because (karena) since (karena) so (sehingga)

5. condition (syarat)

in order that (agar) so that (agar)

6. purpose (tujuan)

as long as (jika, dengan syarat) if (jika, dengan syarat) only if (hanya jika) otherwise (jika tidak) or else (jika tidak) provided /providing (that) (jika, dengan syarata) supposing (asumsikan, anggap) unless (jika tidak)

7. manner (cara)

as if (seperti) as though (seperti) how (bagaimana) the way (cara)

29. Combine the sentences below using "who" the people are trying to purchase a new phone. They are waiting out side the st.


Relative Pronoun

Combine the sentences below using "who" the people are trying to purchase a new phone. They are waiting out side the street.

Jawab:

The people are trying to purchase a new phone.

They are waiting out side the street.

The people who are waiting outside the street are trying to purchase a new phone.

Kata 'who' diatas digunakan untuk menjelaskan subyek noun yaitu the people.

Pembahasan

Kata who diatas merupakan relative pronouns. Relative pronoun adalah kata atau kalimat yang digunakan untuk menggabungkan kalimat dan untuk menjelaskan noun atau pronoun.

Relative pronoun contohnya adalah who, whose, which, whom dan that. Perbedaan pengunaan relative pronouns tersebut adalah :

who untuk menjelaskan orang (subyek)whom digunakan untuk menjelaskan orang (obyek)whose digunakan untuk menjelaskan kepemilikan biasanya digunakan untuk situasi formalwhich digunakan untuk orang/animals/bendathat digunakan untuk orang/animals/benda dan biasanya digunakan untuk situasi informal

Berikut contoh penggunaan relative pronoun dalam kalimat :

I don't like people who interrupted me.She is the student who handwriting is the best in the class.Did you see the woman which wearing a black dress?The boy who likes pizza is her son.That the boy whose mother is on the TV.Pelajari Lebih Lanjut

36. Why Indonesia trying to develop its industry


Jawaban:

Indonesia is trying to develop its industry to boost economic growth, increase employment opportunities, and reduce dependence on foreign imports. By developing its industry, Indonesia can produce more goods and services domestically, which can help lower their trade deficit and increase their export revenue. This can also create new job opportunities and improve the standard of living for Indonesians. Furthermore, developing its industry can increase the country's ability to innovate and compete globally, which can attract foreign investment and contribute to long term sustainable growth.

Indonesia is trying to develop its industry for several reasons:

1. Economic Growth: Developing the industry is crucial for achieving sustained economic growth. It allows for increased production, job creation, and income generation, which contributes to overall economic development.

2. Employment Opportunities: Industrial development provides employment opportunities for the growing population, reducing unemployment rates and poverty levels. It helps absorb the labor force and enhances the standard of living for individuals and families.

3. Export Promotion: A developed industry enables the production of goods and services that can be exported to other countries, contributing to foreign exchange earnings and improving the country's balance of trade.

4. Value Addition: Developing industries allows for the processing and value addition of raw materials, transforming them into finished products. This adds value to the resources and promotes higher-value exports, increasing profitability and competitiveness.

5. Technological Advancement: Industrial development encourages innovation, research, and development, leading to technological advancements. This, in turn, enhances productivity, efficiency, and competitiveness in both domestic and international markets.

6. Diversification of the Economy: Expanding and diversifying the industrial sector helps reduce reliance on specific sectors, such as agriculture or natural resources. It creates a more balanced and resilient economy less vulnerable to fluctuations in global commodity prices.

7. National Development: Developing industries strengthens national sovereignty and self-sufficiency by reducing dependence on imported goods. It promotes domestic production, which is essential for national development and security.

Overall, developing the industry is a strategic goal for Indonesia to achieve sustainable economic growth, create employment opportunities, enhance technological capabilities, and improve the standard of living for its citizens.
