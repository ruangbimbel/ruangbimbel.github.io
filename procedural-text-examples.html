Situation: Gina will visit her friend tonight after dinner. She will bring Tape Cake but she needs a little help to make it. So she asks her mother for the recipes and ingredients. Let’s check the conversation below!

Gina: Hi mom, are you busy?

Mother: Not at all, my dear. What’s up?

Gina: Well, I will visit Tina at her house after dinner tonight. I will make a surprise for her.

Mother: Wow! That’s good. So, tell me what can I do for you?

Gina: I want to make Tape Cake but can’t make it by myself. Would you like to help me, mom?

Mother: Oh, that’s easy. Let’s go to the kitchen.

Gina: Come on!

………………… later

Mother: Here we go. This is the first thing that we have to do. Prepare the ingredients. 300 gr of Tape, 3 eggs, 100 gr of sugar, 100 gr of flour, 100 gr of butter, 100 ml of milk. By the way, does Tina like cheese?

Gina: Yes, she loves cheese so much.

Mother: Oh, that’s good. So we need 50 gr of cheese.

Gina: Alright, I will repeat the ingredients. They are 300 gr of Tape, 3 eggs, 100 gr of sugar, 100 gr of flour, 100 gr of butter, 100 ml of milk, and 50 gr of cheese.

Mother: Yups. That’s right. Let’s go to the next step. Make the Tape smooth and fine.

Gina: Alright. Let me do it.

Mother: Okay. Here you go.

………………… later

Gina: It’s done.

Mother: Let me see. That’s good. Next, mix the Tape with milk.

Gina: Got it! I’ll handle that.

………………… later

Mother: That’s good. The next step, melt the butter in the frying pan.

Gina: Okay. I’ll turn on the stove.

………………… later

Mother: Next, beat the eggs and sugar until 5 minutes or more.

Gina: Yups, I see. Please do it for me, mom.

Mother: My pleasure, dear.

………………… later.

Gina: And what is the next step, mom?

Mother: The next step, mix all ingredients gently with a spatula. Slow but sure. Pour the flour, pour the Tape with milk and pour the butter.

Gina: Okay, let me try, mom.

Mother: Be careful! Do it gently.

Gina: I know, mom. I’m expert at this case. 

………………… later.

Gina: It’s done.

Mother: That’s good.

Gina: What’s next?

Mother: The next step, put it on a baking sheet that has been baked bread paper. Smear butter and sprinkle the flour.

Gina: Let me do that.

………………… later.

Mother: We are going to the final step. Bake with the oven temperature of 200 C until 20 minutes then sprinkle the cheese and continue to bake.

Gina: Alright. I’ll turn on the oven.

Mother: Finally, Tape Cake is ready to serve and ready to enjoy.

Gina: Wow! This is delicious, isn’t it?

15. The generic structure of procedural text is


Here is the generic structures of procedure text :

GENERIC STRUCTURES OF PROCEDURE TEXT :

1. Goal: showing the purpose

2. Material: Telling the needed materials

3. Step 1-end: Describing the steps to achieve the purpose

 

Here is further explanation related to Procedure Text :

What is Procedure Text?

DEFINITION OF PROCEDURE TEXT

Procedure text is a text that is designed to describe how something is achieved through a sequence of actions or steps. It explains how people perform different processes in a sequence of steps.

 

LANGUAGE FEATURE OF PROCEDURE TEXT

 Using temporal conjunction

 Using action verb

 Using imperative sentence

 Using Simple Present Tense

Here is the example of a Procedure Text:

HOW TO MAKE A MILKSHAKE

A milkshake is a sweet, cold beverage which is usually made from milk, ice cream, or iced milk, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate sauce, or fruit syrup. If you want to make a delicious milkshake by your selves, then this is the way how to make it.

 

The ingredients that you need to make a milkshake are:

4 cups vanilla ice cream

2 cups milk

4 teaspoons chocolate sauce (optional)

1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries


Now, follow these steps to make a milkshake:

1.       Place milkshake glasses into the freezer. Using cold glasses will help the shake stay cool to give you time to enjoy. Leave for up to an hour if possible.

2.       Soften ice cream. It is easier to make a shake when the ice cream is slightly soft. Take the ice cream out of the freezer a few minutes before making your shake.

3.       Place the ingredients into a blender. Mix the ice cream, milk and added flavors into the blender. Blend on high for one minute. Don’t leave in the blender for longer or the shake will melt quickly.

4.       Pour shakes into frosty glasses. Take the glasses out of the freezer as soon as the blending is complete. Then pour the shake mixture in.

5.       Drop a straw into the shake, then enjoy. You can sip a shake right from the glass but somehow the straw adds to the flavor and the experience.

If you do not have a blender, you may substitute with a mixer, but be careful as it could be messy. And if you do not have anything else to mix, just use a spoon, but let the ice cream thaw first to make the milkshake easier to mix. Enjoy your milkshake.


Penjelasan dari text prosedur ( PROCEDURE TEXT) di atas :

Paragraf pertama: the Goal. Penulis berusaha menjelaskan apa itu milkshake dan mengapa ia menulis tulisan ini, yaitu ingin memberitahu pembaca bagaimana cara membuat milkshake.

Paragraf kedua: the Materials Needed. Berisi bahan-bahan yang harus dipersiapkan dan dibutuhkan untuk membuat milkshake.

Paragraf ketiga: Steps, yang berisi langkah-langkah yang dishare oleh penulis untuk membuat milkshake dari langkah pertama hingga menjadi milkshake.

40. apa yang di maksud dengan procedural text


Ada tiga definisi "umum" mengenai procedure text : (1)Texts that explain how something works or how to use instruction / operation manuals e.g. how to use the video, the computer, the tape recorder, the photocopier, the fax. (2) Texts that instruct how to do a particular activity e.g. recipes, rules for games, science experiments, road safety rules. (3) Texts that deal with human behaviour eg how to live happily, how to succeed.
