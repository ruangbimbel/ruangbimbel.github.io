PEMBAHASAN

Trigonometri merupakan ilmu matematika yang mempelajari hubungan antara panjang dan sudut pada segitiga. Perbandingan trigonometri pada segitiga siku siku adalah sebagai berikut :

[tex]\displaystyle{sin\theta=\frac{sisi~depan}{sisi~miring}}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{cos\theta=\frac{sisi~samping}{sisi~miring}}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{tan\theta=\frac{sisi~depan}{sisi~samping}}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{cosec\theta=\frac{1}{sin\theta}=\frac{sisi~miring}{sisi~depan}}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{sec\theta=\frac{1}{cos\theta}=\frac{sisi~miring}{sisi~samping}}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{cot\theta=\frac{1}{tan\theta}=\frac{sisi~samping}{sisi~depan}}[/tex]

.

DIKETAHUI

[tex]sin\alpha=0,75[/tex]

.

DITANYA

Tentukan nilai dari :

a. cosα.

b. tanα.

c. cosecα.

.

PENYELESAIAN

[tex]sin\alpha=0,75[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{\frac{sisi~depan}{sisi~miring}=\frac{3}{4}}[/tex]

[tex]sisi~samping=\sqrt{(sisi~miring)^2-(sisi~depan)^2}[/tex]

[tex]sisi~samping=\sqrt{(4)^2-(3)^2}[/tex]

[tex]sisi~samping=\sqrt{7}[/tex]

.

Soal a.

[tex]\displaystyle{cos\alpha=\frac{sisi~samping}{sisi~miring} }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{cos\alpha=\frac{\sqrt{7}}{4} }[/tex]

.

Soal b.

[tex]\displaystyle{tan\alpha=\frac{sisi~depan}{sisi~samping} }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{tan\alpha=\frac{3}{\sqrt{7}}\times\frac{\sqrt{7}}{\sqrt{7}} }[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{tan\alpha=\frac{3}{7}\sqrt{7} }[/tex]

.

Soal c.

[tex]\displaystyle{cosec\alpha=\frac{1}{sin\alpha}}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle{cosec\alpha=\frac{4}{3}}[/tex]

.

KESIMPULAN

a. Nilai dari cosα adalah [tex]\displaystyle{\boldsymbol{\frac{\sqrt{7}}{4}} }[/tex].

b. Nilai dari tanα adalah [tex]\displaystyle{\boldsymbol{\frac{3}{7}\sqrt{7}} }[/tex].

PEMBAHASAN

RECOUNT TEXT

Recount Text adalah salah satu jenis text dalam bahasa Inggris yang menceritakan kembali tentang kejadian-kejadian atau pengalaman-pengalaman di masa lampau. Tujuan dari Recount Text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau untuk menghibur pembaca sehingga tidak terdapat konflik.  

Ada beberapa jenis recount text, contohnya adalah diaries, letters/postcards, journals, autobiographies dan historical recount. nah text di atas adalah salah satu contoh personal recount.

Text organization:

Orientation : berisi tentang pengenalan mengenai latar belakang cerita, kapan , dimana dan orang-orang yang trerlibat di dalam cerita Event :merupakan rangkaian kejadian di dalam cerita yang di ceritakan secara kronologis Reorientation : kesimpulan yang merupakan komentar penulis mengenai kejadian.

Unsur Kebahasaan:

Menggunakan noun dan pronoun, contoh: Bung Tomo, weapons, soldiers, we, they Menggunakan action verbs , contoh: went, played, spent Menggunakan time conjunction, contoh: and, but, after, dan finally Menggunakan adverbs dan adverbs of phrases, contoh: in my house, two days ago, slowly Menggunakan adjective, contoh: brave, strong, smart   Mrnggunakan Simple Past Tense

Berikut penjelasan tentang Simple Past Tense:

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist ?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent?

10. (+) My father was in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office  yesterday afternoon?
