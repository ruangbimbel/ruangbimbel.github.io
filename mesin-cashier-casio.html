30. The cashier always … the customers well A.SERVE B. SERVES


b.serves

semoga membantuAnswer: 

The sentence above is  in present simple tense. The subject is 'The cashier' which is one of the singular subjects, 'he', 'she' and 'it; so the verb is added by suffix -es/-s. Thus, the answer is
The cashier always serves the customers well.

Hopefully the explanation is quite clear for you.

Mapel: English
Kategori: present simple
