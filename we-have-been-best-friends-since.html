5. buatlah dalam bentuk kalimat negatif 1. we have been doing our homework 2. Rahmad has been talking about his planning. 3. I have been meeting many people since i come here


Present Perfect Continuous

1. (+)  We have been doing our homework

(-) We haven't been doing our homework

(?) Have we been doing our homework?

2. (+) Rahmad has been talking about his planning.

(-) Rahmad hasn't been talking about his planning.

(?) Has Rahmad been talking about his planning?

3. (+) I have been meeting many people since I came here.

(-) I haven't been meeting many people since I came here

(?) Have I been meeting many people since I came here?

Explanation

Present Perfect Continuous tense merupakan verb tense yang digunakan dalam menjabarkan kejadian yang telah berlangsung di waktu lampau, belum diselesaikan, dan masih terdapat kelanjutannya sampai saat ini.

Penanda kata dari penggunaan present perfect continuous tense, yaitu recently, lately, still, just, ever, never, since, for.

Konstruksi rumus Present perfect continuous tense :

(+) S+has/have+been+verb-ing+ O/C

(-) S+has/have+not+been+verb-ing+O/C

(?) Has/have+S+been+verb-ing+O/C

Penempatan keterangan pada present perfect continuous tense hanya pada kalimat positive dan tanya :

(+) S+has/have+adverb+been+verb-ing+O/C

(?) Has/have+S+adverb+been+verb-ing+O/C

Penggunaan auxiliary verb "Has" & "Have" :

She, he, it, nama orang dan kata benda tunggal=> has

I, they, we, you dan kata benda jamak=> have

Example :

(+) They have been waiting a bus for almost two hours

(+) The committee has just been discussing for twenty minutes

(-)  He has not been doing his tasks.

(?) Have you been working hard lately ?
