12. Ubahlah kalimat berikut kedalam simple past tense, past proressive tense, dan past perfect tense1. Was talking on the phone at that time 2. She put a camera in my locker yesterday 3. We had a family gathering on the last lebaran day​


Jawaban:

Simple past tense:

Talked on the phone at that time.She put a camera in my locker yesterday.We had a family gathering on the last lebaran day.

Past progressive tense:

Was talking on the phone at that time.She was putting a camera in my locker yesterday.We were having a family gathering on the last lebaran day.

Past perfect tense:

Had been talking on the phone at that time.She had put a camera in my locker before I arrived.We had already had a family gathering by the end of the last lebaran day.

Penjelasan:

untuk megubah menjadi simple past tense bisa menggunakan rumus:

(S + Verb 2 + O/C)

untuk megubah menjadi past progressive tense bisa menggunakan rumus:

(S + was/were + Verb I + ing + O/C)

untuk megubah menjadi Past perfect tense bisa menggunakan rumus:

(S + had + Verb 3 + O/C)
