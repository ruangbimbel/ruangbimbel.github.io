17. Sinopsis the ant and the Dove


The Ant and the Dove

One hot day, an ant was seeking for some water. After walking around for a moment, she came to a spring. To reach the spring, she had to climb up a blade of grass. While making her way up, she slipped and fell unintentionally into the water.

She could have sunk if a dove up a nearby tree had not seen her. Seeing that the ant was in trouble, the dove quickly put off a leaf from a tree and dropped it immediately into the water near the struggling ant. Then the ant moved towards the leaf and climbed up there. Soon it carried her safely to dry ground.

Not long after at that, there was a hunter nearby who was throwing out his net towards the dove, hoping to trap it in this way.

Guessing what he should do, the ant quickly bit him on the heel. Feeling the pain, the hunter dropped his net and the dove flew away quickly from this net.

The morality: One good turn deserves another.

40. The ant and the dove generic structure


Orientatin

One hot day, an ant was searching for some water. After walking around for some time, she came to a spring.

An ant => Specific participant

One hot day => circumstances of time

After => temporal conjunction

Complication (1)

To reach the spring she had to climb up a blade of grass. While making her way up, she slipped and fell into the water.

the spring => circumstances of place

and => coordinate conjunction

Resolution (1)

She could have drowned if a dove up a nearby tree had not helped her. Seeing that the ant was in trouble, the dove quickly plucked a leaf and dropped it into the water near the struggling ant. The ant moved towards the leaf and climbed up there. Soon, it carried her safely to dry ground.

And => coordinate conjunction

Moved => past tense

Complication (2)

Just as that time, a hunter nearby was throwing out his net towards the dove, hoping to trap it.

Was => linking verb

Resolution (2)

Guessing what he was about to do, the ant quickly bit him on the heel. Feeling the pain, the hunter dropped his net. Quickly, the dove flied away to safety.

Dropped => action verb

Was => linking verb
