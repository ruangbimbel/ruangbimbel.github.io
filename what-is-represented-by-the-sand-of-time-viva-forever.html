1. What is represented by the sand of time !


The sands of time is an English idiom relating the passage of time to the sand in an hourglass

The image of the sand being emptied in the hourglass creates a visual metaphor for the limited duration of human life, and for the inevitability of change in the world as a whole

6. The truck could carry some of the sand. The correct passive form of the above sentence is .....


Jawaban:

c.

The correct passive form of the above sentence is .....

Some of the sand could be carried by the truck.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas adalah kalimat modals (could).

Rumus passive modals adalah

Object + modals + be + verb 3 + by subject.

Pada kalimat di atas:

The truck (subject) could (modal) carry (verb 1) some of the sand (object).

Passive: some of the sand (object) could (modal) be carried (verb 3) by the truck (subject).

Arti dari "a chronicle represented in order of" adalah sebuah kronik (catatan peristiwa menurut urutan waktu kejadiannya) disajikan urutan).
